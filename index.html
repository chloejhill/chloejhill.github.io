I work with government organisations, NGOs and the private sector to create meaningful content and robust strategies that help to instigate impactful change.

My journey

What?

Sustainability has always been my passion and my purpose. After studying the ‘socks off it’ at University with three degrees (BSc, MSc and PhD) under my belt, I then ventured across four different continents over 15 years accumulating essential experience, from the jungles of Tanzania, the reefs of Indonesia, the cloudforests of Ecuador to the protected areas of Costa Rica and Panama, the shores of Lake Geneva and the Mekong Delta in Vietnam and Cambodia.

How?

Thanks to my technical background and applied multi-sectoral international experience, combined with my thirst for creativity using an array of mediums and my desire to inspire change and deliver lasting and sustainable impact across different audiences, today my focus is geared towards offering clients a delicate blend of well grounded yet creatively crafted sustainability communications to drive that all important behaviour change.

With whom?

I’ve collaborated with organizations at the international, regional, national and grassroots level - from the United Nations to Governments, Development Banks, NGOs, local community groups and social enterprises - working on all different facets of sustainability.
