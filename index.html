Hello! I'm Chloe.

I’m a creative strategist & content designer working in the field of sustainability.


I work with government organisations, NGOs and the private sector to create meaningful content and robust strategies that help to instigate impactful change.

What I do

Dig Deep: Understanding the challenge

  • Scope and scan
  • Research - audience mapping, interviews, personal observations, document analyses, future thinking/forecasting etc
  • Diagnostics and establishing needs and priorities

Get Creative: Developing ideas, visions and strategies

  • Co-create with the client
  • Visualize, ideate & innovate
  • Build strategy & design content for any platform

Deliver Impact: Instigating the change

  • Provide roll out guidance
  • Storytelling and communication
  • Stakeholder engagement for maximum outreach

Cross check: Monitoring progress & Maintaining quality

  • Sustainability reporting
  • Ongoing editorial and strategic support from copy editing new text to strategic communications guidance.

What I love

Messaging for Sustainability

I love crafting messages that matter and helping my clients communicate to the world their exciting journey towards sustainability.

Visioning the Future

I love looking at future trends in the sustainability realm and how this can impact on organization’s trajectories, product designs and service delivery.

Creating an Impact

I love making sustainability relevant in such a way that generates positive impact and creates behaviour change amongst a range of different audiences.

My journey

What?

Sustainability has always been my passion and my purpose. After studying the ‘socks off it’ at University with three degrees (BSc, MSc and PhD) under my belt, I then ventured across four different continents accumulating essential experience in this sector, from the jungles of Tanzania to the protected areas of Costa Rica and Panama to the Mekong Delta in Vietnam and Cambodia to the shores of Lake Geneva.

With whom?

I’ve collaborated with organizations at the international, regional, national and grassroots level - from the United Nations to Governments, Development Banks, NGOs, local community groups and social enterprises - working on all different facets of sustainability.

How?

Thanks to my technical grounding, my objective is to work with clients to strategize, design compelling content, and deliver creative communications and story tell to instigate change and deliver lasting and sustainable impact.

You can see of my dedicated writing and strategic communications work here in my portfolio.
